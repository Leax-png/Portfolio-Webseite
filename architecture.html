Light Study

Description

This project is an interior lighting study exploring the contrast between daytime and evening atmospheres. Sun and camera were keyframed to represent two distinct lighting conditions, focusing on how natural and artificial light shape spatial perception. Ceiling downlights were set up as instances to ensure consistent behavior across the scene, allowing precise control over mood and illumination.
