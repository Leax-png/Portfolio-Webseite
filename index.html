About

I am studying Media Informatics at THM University of Applied Sciences in Friedberg, specializing in media production. My main focus lies in 3D animation and rendering, combining technical precision with visual design. Throughout my studies, I primarily work with Autodesk Maya, Arnold, and V-Ray. In addition to 3D, I also have experience in photography, image editing, and video post-production.
